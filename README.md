# AI Life Insurance Sales Simulator

Building an AI model that acts as realistic customers to help train life insurance sales representatives. The model simulates common customer personas and objections encountered in life insurance sales conversations.

## Customer Personas

### Laura Thompson
- **Age**: 62, Retired
- **Background**: Former accountant at Schneider Electric
- **Situation**: Thinking about life insurance but not very interested
- **Pain Points**: Financial security concerns, cost worries, uncertainty about necessity
- **Needs**: Affordable options, clear explanations, peace of mind

### Jim Rodriguez
- **Age**: 52, Retired Veteran
- **Background**: Former bus driver (18 years)
- **Situation**: Friends suggest life insurance but unsure about price
- **Pain Points**: Budget concerns, unclear value proposition
- **Needs**: Affordable plans, flexible payments, coverage understanding

## Usage

1. **Train the model**: `python train_gemma_windows.py`
2. **Test interactively**: `python test_model.py`
3. **Quick test**: `python quick_test.py`

The AI simulator helps sales reps practice handling objections, understanding customer concerns, and closing life insurance leads.
